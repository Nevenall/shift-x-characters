Determination & Doom

Damage & Healing

When hit take damage equal to the attack rating − durability (+ resistance, if applicable). If the damage is > 0 you are taken out of the scene unless you absorb it all.

– Mark a stress box to absorb 1 damage

– Take a consequence to absorb 2, 4, or 6 damage.

Recover all stress at the end of a scene.

Make Stamina or Psyche roll to start recovery and then wait:

Mild​—One full scene after treatment

Moderate​—One full session after treatment

Severe​—Clear when you reach a breakthrough, after treatment

Support

You may use one power or rating, in an action but your others, and other heroes, can provide extra support.

Powers. A power with a positive rating can support your action by granting a +1 provided you can narrate your action in such a way that the power could help. If the power is Amazing (+6) or better, it provides +2 support.

Ratings. A positive rating can support your action by granting a +1 if you narrate your action in such a way that the rating can apply. If the rating is Amazing (+6) or better, it provides +2 support.

Help. Other heroes may support your action with their powers following the same pattern.
