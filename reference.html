Terms

Assets are durable details introduced into the narrative. They have their own ratings and last for the rest of the scene.

Advantages are temporary +2 bonuses with a flavor tag. They last until after your next action. They can be passed to allies.

A call on is allowed for some traits, such as distinctions. When your trait matches the narrative circumstances you may spend a determination and call on that trait make a second roll and keep the better result. Alternatively, if you've succeeded on a roll you may gain a determination if roll again and keep the worst result.

Determination Our name for Fate Points. A metacurrency used for bonuses and gained with narrative bad stuff.

Distinction A trait characters have

Trait is a generic word for a mechanical element a character might possess.

Opposition is the rating that opposes your actions. Based on what you are attempting, the narrative circumstances, and villains who oppose you.

SWS Succeed with Style. A critical hit. You achieve something beyond your intent.
