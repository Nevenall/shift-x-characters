 Powerset—Miss Fortune

Thanks to your dad's shenanigans, the women in your family are cursed with terrible luck but Manos is teaching you how to control it.

Powers:

   Misfortune.  Elemental Control: Misfortune

Limits:

   Shared Misfortune. Controlling misfortune is always at least a Risky action.

   The Curse. When you roll a 6 or better to resist, shutdown Miss Fortune until you add a villain die, or take a transition action.

Powerset—Rodeo Girl

You've been roping and riding since you could walk. 

Powers:

   My Papa's Pistols. Attack

   Constant Training. Reflexes

   Rope Tricks. Stretching

Sfx:

   Rope 'n Ride.  Advantage for rodeo related actions such as roping, riding, and horse racing.

   Trick Shot. When you push yourself shooting your pistols you may also perform a superhuman feat such as shooting a bullet out of the air or arching a shot into a moving target miles away. 

Limits:

   Gear. Your pistols and rope are gear. They can be broken, stolen, or run out of ammo.
